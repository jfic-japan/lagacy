UCI 国際コミセールコースの案内が届いています。

JCFを通じて、UCI 国際コミセールコースの案内が届いています。
申し込みの締切はJCFを通じて4/3(日)です。興味のある方は至急、事務局までご連絡ください。

Training course for UCI Indoor International Commissaires 2016

Course details:

Venue: Winterthur – Switzerland
Dates: 10th – 12th June 2016 (3days)
Arrival and Departure : arrival Friday before 12:00 – Departure Sunday after 13:00
Language: English
Provisional programme will be sent later

To be able to be admitted to the selection procedure to become UCI Indoor International Commissaire, the candidate has to meet the following conditions:

1) be a licence holder of a UCI-affiliated National Federation.
2) have an excellent knowledge of the UCI Regulations.
3) evidence that he/she has served actively as a National Commissaire in the two years preceding the selection.
4) have a command of the official course language English.
5) be proposed by the National Federation. This Federation is required to submit an application signed by its President or a person delegated by its President which shall include the following documentation:
・ UCI registration form
・ a copy of an official identity document (ID or passport) showing that the candidate is aged between 25 and 50 years in the year of his selection by the UCI
・ a certificate or diploma of Indoor national commissaire.

The UCI operates a final selection, based on the files received and the places available. The parties concerned will be informed once the selection has been made in April. We thank you for precising if the participant is interested in the Artistic cycling or Cycle-ball discipline.

Meantime we would like to remind that all the participants will be responsible for their traveling costs, accommodation and meals during the course. However, the UCI will offer them the most favourable arrangement. The participants will receive more detailed information on due time.
